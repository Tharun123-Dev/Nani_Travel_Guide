Why these picks?

Curated from major travel roundups and trend reports (Lonely Planet, Travel+Leisure, Google/Skyscanner trends). Each card below includes a short trip idea and a photo — click any image to enlarge.

Kyoto — Japan (2025)

Culture & temples • easy city-to-nature day trips

Autumn leaves, thousand torii gates and world-class food make Kyoto a top choice for culture seekers. Combine with Osaka or Hiroshima for a full Kansai trip.

Lisbon — Portugal

History • cliffs & coastline • multi-city feed

Warm, walkable streets, pastel buildings and great seafood. Lisbon is a gateway to Algarve beaches and day trips to Sintra's palaces.

New Zealand

Epic landscapes • outdoor adventures

From fiords to volcanic deserts, NZ remains a top nature destination for hiking, kayaking and starry-night escapes — perfect for active travelers.

Iceland

Northern lights • dramatic coasts

Geothermal pools, volcanic landscapes and the aurora borealis — Iceland is ideal for photographers and nature lovers seeking otherworldly scenery.

Machu Picchu — Peru

Ancient ruins • Andean treks

Combine the Inca Trail or shorter hikes with Cusco's markets and Peruvian cuisine for a cultural-highland experience.

Santorini — Greece

Sunset views • island life

Iconic sunsets, volcanic beaches and cliffside villages. Great for a romantic getaway or island-hopping itinerary in the Aegean.
